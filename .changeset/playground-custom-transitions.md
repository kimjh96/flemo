Add seven custom playground transitions, selectable from the transition picker. Five are pure `createTransition` demos — `zoom` (cross-zoom dive), `card-stack` (iOS sheet present with a receding backdrop), `reveal` (clip-path iris that opens to just cover the viewport), and `spring` (overshooting bounce). Three more combine a custom `createDecorator` layer applied to the backgrounding screen: `sheet` (rising sheet + dark dim, an iOS modal present), `spotlight` (the new screen grows from a small centered card while a radial vignette darkens the backdrop's edges), and `drawer` (cupertino-style slide with a brand-blue tint over the receding screen).
